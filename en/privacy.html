Privacy Policy

How personal data is handled when visiting this website or sending a direct booking request.

Data controller

Casa Vacanze Fiore Ciampino

Email: casafioreciampino@gmail.com

Data processed

  • Contact data provided by the user (e.g., first name, last name, email, phone, message content), including through the direct booking form (which prepares an email).
  • Request details (e.g., number of guests, check-in/check-out dates, optional notes).
  • Technical data (e.g., IP address and navigation logs) generated by normal website usage and hosting services.

How direct booking works

The direct booking form does not process online payments and does not create a booking automatically. It prepares an email message with the details you entered, which is then sent via your email client (or you can write directly to casafioreciampino@gmail.com).

Purposes

  • Responding to information requests sent by email.
  • Handling direct booking enquiries (availability checks, quotes, confirmation and stay organization).
  • Ensuring website security and technical operation.

Legal basis

  • Pre-contractual measures: managing enquiries before a possible stay contract.
  • Legitimate interest: website security and prevention of abuse.
  • Consent: where required (e.g., explicit acceptance of the policy via checkbox).

Recipients

Data is processed by the controller and may be processed by technical providers (e.g., website hosting, email provider) acting as service providers.

External links and third-party services

This website links to external platforms (e.g., Booking, Airbnb, VRBO). Their own privacy policies apply. Embedded third-party content (e.g., YouTube and Google Maps) may also collect data according to their policies.

Retention

Data shared by email (including booking requests) is kept as long as necessary to manage the request and for organizational/administrative needs, then deleted or anonymized when no longer needed.

Your rights

You can request access, updates, restriction, or deletion of your data by writing to casafioreciampino@gmail.com.
